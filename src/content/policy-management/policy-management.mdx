# Policy Management

By leveraging Cloudsmith's Policy Management, organizations can streamline their security operations, maintain compliance with software licensing requirements, and proactively mitigate potential threats, thereby safeguarding their software supply chain and maintaining the integrity of their software artifacts.

Cloudsmith's Policy Management capabilities cover authentication policies, license policies, vulnerability policies, and deny list policies.
