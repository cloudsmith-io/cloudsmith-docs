# Log In

Learn how to access your Cloudsmith [Workspaces](/workspaces).

## Username and password

Go to [https://app.cloudsmith.com/login](https://app.cloudsmith.com/login) and enter your e-mail address and password. You'll be taken to your Workspace overview page.

## SAML

Go to [https://app.cloudsmith.com/login/saml](https://app.cloudsmith.com/login/saml) or click **Login using SAML** on the main login page.

Enter your **workspace name** in the form and click **Continue**.

<Note variant="warning" headline="SAML and Primary Workspace">
To use SAML, your workspace must be set as your primary workspace. See [primary workspace](/workspaces#primary-workspace) for learn more.
</Note>

Complete any required MFA steps required by your administrator. 

## Device verification

When signing in from a new device, you may be asked to verify it.  
Click the verification link sent to your email.
